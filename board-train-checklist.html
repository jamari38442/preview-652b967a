A few packing tips

  • Label everything. Bowls, leashes, and beds all start to look the same after the first few dogs.
  • Bring more food than you think you need. Extending a stay is easier than an emergency food run.
  • Skip anything with sentimental value that you'd be upset to lose or see chewed.

What happens after drop-off

Once your dog is settled in, we'll start building the foundation for whichever program you booked, whether that's on-leash basics, off-leash reliability, or a full behavior modification plan. You'll get updates along the way, and a hands-on handoff lesson before your dog comes home so the training actually transfers to you.
