Canine Intuition (in·tu·i·tion)

With over a decade of experience and over 1,500 dogs evaluated, rehabilitated, and trained, Canine Intuition brings a balanced approach to teaching and training both the dog and its owner throughout the South Florida area.

We don’t believe in one-size-fits-all. Every program is customized to your dog’s temperament and your lifestyle, so the training actually sticks once we’re gone.
